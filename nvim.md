## keybindings
* lsp: `SPC l `
* toggles:      `SPC t`
* buffer: `SPC b`
* nerdTree: `SPC n`
* comments: `SPC c`
* save: `C-s`
* close: `C-x C-v`
### lsp
* start completion: `C-SPC`
* popup: `SPC l h`
* complete:
  * choose: `C-n` and `C-p`
  * insert: `Enter`
* errors: `SPC l e`
